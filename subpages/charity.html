What is The Nature Conservancy ?

The Nature Conservancy is charitable environmental non-profit organization, which is trying to protect the natural environment.

Animals are haunted and the nature is robbed everyday of her beautiful wealth. For example , the 9,700 species of birds in the world, almost half of them, about 4,500 breed in the Americas. They are exposed to constant risk.

The organization has already protected over 119,000,000 acres (48,000,000 ha) of land and thousands of miles of rivers worldwide.

Over last years they expanded international conservation efforts include work in North America, Central America, and South America, Africa, the Pacific Rim, the Caribbean, and Asia.

It`s easy to ignore the fact that the earth is in danger. The time of change has come. Be part of it with us.
